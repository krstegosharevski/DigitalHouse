About Digital House

Digital House is a proud, officially licensed partner of Telekom.mk, acting as a bridge between our customers and the world-renowned telecommunications provider, T-Mobile. With a strong commitment to delivering quality, convenience, and excellent customer service, Digital House brings the latest in mobile technology, broadband services, and T-Mobile solutions to the heart of Resen.

As an authorized intermediary, we offer a comprehensive selection of T-Mobile products and services, including the latest smartphones, accessories, high-speed internet plans, and customized tariff solutions for both individuals and businesses. Our experienced team ensures that every customer receives the best guidance to meet their needs, whether they are upgrading their devices, exploring new data plans, or troubleshooting any service-related concerns.

Our strategic location in the city center of Resen makes it easy and convenient for locals to access premium telecommunications products and services without the need to travel far. Digital House’s store is designed to give customers an interactive experience, allowing them to explore the latest devices, ask questions, and receive personalized assistance from our knowledgeable team.

Being a trusted intermediary of T-Mobile, we handle all service activations, upgrades, and troubleshooting exclusively through Telekom.mk, ensuring the highest standards of service, security, and reliability. Every product and service offered by Digital House is backed by T-Mobile’s commitment to excellence, giving our customers peace of mind and the latest in connectivity solutions.

Our mission at Digital House is to provide seamless access to T-Mobile’s world-class services while delivering a personalized, local touch. We are here to help you connect, explore, and achieve more with the best telecommunications solutions available today. Whether you're looking to stay connected with friends and family, enhance your business's communication capabilities, or simply explore the latest in mobile technology, Digital House is here to make it happen.
